Skilled Worker Visa

Sponsorship Licence to employ workers from outside of the UK

Add your Skilled Worker Visa overview here.

Sponsor licence requirements

  • Be genuine, operating, and trading lawfully in the UK
  • Be UK-based
  • Not represent a threat to immigration control
  • Have key personnel who are “honest and dependable”
  • Have adequate HR systems to meet sponsor duties

UK Global Talent Visa

An unsponsored work route for leaders and potential leaders

Add your Global Talent overview here.

UK Global Talent Visa Requirements

  • Be aged 18 or over
  • Hold an endorsement from an approved endorsing body (or eligible prize route)
  • Meet general grounds (no refusal reasons)
  • TB certificate may be required depending on country

UK Visitor Visa

Travel to the UK for short stays and permitted activities

Add your Visitor Visa overview here.

How long does a UK visitor visa last?

  • Standard Visitor Visa usually allows up to 6 months per visit
  • Long-term visitor visas may be available (2, 5, or 10 years)
  • You normally can’t stay longer than 6 months at a time
